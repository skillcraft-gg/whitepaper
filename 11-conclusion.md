## Conclusion

Skillcraft turns a familiar problem into a practical architecture.
Developers can move quickly with AI, while proof generation and policy evaluation make that activity inspectable.

The core contribution is a clean chain: capture in git, evaluate by policy, publish by index.
The current codebase already implements that chain end-to-end for CLI capture, proof verification, credential policy evaluation, and public skills/credentials exposure.

Current status in practice is:
- Implemented: local evidence capture, commit trailer proof references, deterministic claim processing, and active `/skills` and `/credentials` pages.
- Roadmap item: expand public publication surfaces beyond the current `/skills` and `/credentials` set; loadouts is on that roadmap.

Skillcraft already delivers a practical, open platform for verifiable capability with a high-signal trust chain, and the roadmap continues to expand discoverability where needed.
