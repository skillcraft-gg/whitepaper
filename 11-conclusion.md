## Conclusion

Skillcraft turns a familiar problem into a practical architecture.
Developers can move quickly with AI, while proof generation and policy evaluation make that activity inspectable.

The core contribution is not a new social layer.
It is a clean chain: capture in git, evaluate by policy, publish by index.
That chain already exists in the current codebase, with clear next steps for completion.

The immediate priority is finishing the composition and publication surfaces so users can fully exercise the model end to end.
Once loadouts are populated and routes are complete, the ecosystem reaches a much stronger public posture: not just a working internals model, but a usable open platform for verifiable capability.
